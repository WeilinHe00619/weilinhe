About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Bristol, where I am supervised by Dr. Cristina David and Prof. Meng Wang. My research journey is driven by a passion for bridging the gap between cutting-edge artificial intelligence and rigorous software engineering principles.

Research Interests

Neuro-Symbolic Reasoning

Combining LLMs' generative power with symbolic logic for verifiable code synthesis.

Correctness-Guaranteed GenAI

Developing frameworks to ensure behavioral consistency in automatically generated code.

Early Error Detection

Leveraging formal verification for incomplete programs to improve generation efficiency.

Scalable Modular Generation

Managing complex module dependencies and dependency graphs in large-scale codebases.
