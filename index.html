About Me

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Bristol (commencing 2025), having previously completed my MSc in Data Science at the same institution. My academic journey began at Tianjin University, where I earned my BSc in Information System and Information Management. My research interests lie at the intersection of Large Language Models (LLMs), programming languages, and data science, with a particular focus on uncertainty estimation and code generation correctness.
