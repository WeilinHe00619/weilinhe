About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Bristol, supervised by Dr. Cristina David and Prof. Meng Wang. My passion for research is fuelled by my conviction that I am engaged in meaningful work with the potential to make a real impact on society.

Research Interests

  • 01 Neuro-Symbolic Reasoning
    Combining LLMs' generative power with symbolic logic for verifiable code synthesis.
  • 02 Correctness-Guaranteed GenAI
    Developing frameworks to ensure behavioral consistency in automatically generated code.
  • 03 Early Error Detection
    Leveraging formal verification for incomplete programs to improve generation efficiency.
  • 04 Scalable Modular Generation
    Managing complex module dependencies and dependency graphs in large-scale codebases.
